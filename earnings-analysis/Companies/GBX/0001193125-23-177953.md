## Attribution

### Primary Driver
**Massive Adjusted EPS Beat (+75.86%)**
- Surprise: Expected $0.58, Got $1.02 (+$0.44, or +75.86%)
- Evidence: News headline states "Greenbrier Companies Q3 Adj EPS $1.02 Beats $0.58 Estimate... by 75.86 percent" (Source: News, 2023-06-29T06:01:22)
- Why it matters: The EPS beat was driven by manufacturing efficiencies "materialized more quickly than expected" (CEO Lorie Tekorius, Transcript Prepared Remarks). Manufacturing gross margin improved 260bps sequentially to 9.6%, with supply chain headwinds "largely in the rearview mirror."

### Contributing Factor #1
**Revenue Beat (+15.49%)**
- Evidence: Revenue of $1.038B vs $898.75M estimate (Source: News "Sales $1.04B Beat $898.75M Estimate", 2023-06-29)
- Impact: Reinforced the EPS beat narrative; demonstrates top-line growth supporting margin expansion

### Contributing Factor #2
**FY23 Guidance Raise**
- Evidence: Revenue guidance raised from $3.4B-$3.7B to $3.8B-$3.9B, beating $3.68B consensus (Source: News "Greenbrier Boosts FY23 Revenue Forecast", 2023-06-29)
- Impact: Forward-looking signal that operational improvements are sustainable

### Contributing Factor #3
**11% Dividend Increase**
- Evidence: Dividend raised from $0.27 to $0.30 per share (Source: 8-K EX-99.1, News "Greenbrier Board Increases Quarterly Dividend By 11%", 2023-06-29)
- Impact: Signal of management confidence in cash flow sustainability; 37th consecutive quarterly dividend

### Contributing Factor #4
**Strong Post-Quarter Order Activity**
- Evidence: 7,900 units valued at $975M ordered after quarter end (Source: 8-K EX-99.1, 2023-06-29)
- Impact: Provides visibility and confidence in FY24 production; backlog now effectively ~31,300 units

### Contributing Factor #5
**Analyst Response**
- Evidence: Wells Fargo maintained Overweight, raised price target from $37 to $48 (Source: News, 2023-06-30)
- Impact: Professional validation of the beat-and-raise narrative
