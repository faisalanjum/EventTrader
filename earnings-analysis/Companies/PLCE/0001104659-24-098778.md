## Executive Summary

The Children's Place (PLCE) surged **+85.13%** on September 11, 2024, after reporting Q2 FY2024 results that showed a dramatic return to profitability. The primary driver was a **massive EPS beat**: the company reported adjusted EPS of **$0.30** versus a consensus estimate of **-$1.10**, a swing of $1.40 per share from expected loss to actual profit (+127.27% surprise).

Despite a minor revenue miss (-2.36%), the market rewarded the company's successful cost restructuring, with gross margins reaching 35% and SG&A at 15-year lows. This was the first profitable quarter after two years of consecutive losses, triggering a circuit breaker trading halt due to extreme upside volatility.

### EPS Surprise Calculation

```
Actual Adjusted EPS: $0.30
Consensus EPS: -$1.10
Surprise = (($0.30 - (-$1.10)) / |-$1.10|) x 100
Surprise = ($1.40 / $1.10) x 100 = +127.27%
```

### Revenue Surprise Calculation

```
Actual Revenue: $319.655M
Consensus Revenue: $327.380M
Surprise = (($319.655M - $327.380M) / $327.380M) x 100
Surprise = (-$7.725M / $327.380M) x 100 = -2.36%
```

2. **Revenue**: -2.36% (minor, overshadowed)
